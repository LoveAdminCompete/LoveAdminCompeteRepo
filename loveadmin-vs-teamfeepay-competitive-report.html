Positioning comparison

LoveAdmin

Comprehensive administration platform for sport & activity organisations covering membership, automated payments, events, communications, reporting and growth enablement. Transparent plan information is publicly listed, with a clearly documented platform fee and implementation/services noted on pricing pages.

Refs: Pricing Plans • Payment solution features

TeamFeePay

Football‑focused club management suite spanning registrations, payments, shop, fundraising, governance, facilities and club development resources, plus mobile apps. Pricing page lists monthly/annual licence; payment processing fees are fixed per‑transaction and vary by plan; additional policy items include reserve account and disputes fee.

Refs: Homepage • Pricing • Client Terms

Key gaps between the two platforms

Click headers to sort
Theme Gap Description Actionable Take for LoveAdmin Evidence
Transparency TFP payment processing fees are not itemised publicly and vary by plan; default schedule only on request. Keep fee structure transparent and surface example cost calculators in‑product and on web pages. TFP T&Cs §13.15–13.17
Cashflow & Risk TFP requires a reserve account; disputes may incur up to £50 fee. Coach sales teams to contrast no reserve requirement and offer guidance on managing chargebacks. TFP T&Cs §13.18–13.21
Comms Depth Some TFP users report limited ability to email members within the system. Lead with LA’s targeting & scheduling; publish comms playbooks for clubs migrating from manual email. SoftwareAdvice reviews
Fundraising TFP markets football‑specific fundraising mini‑games. Explore lightweight fundraising add‑ons or integrations to neutralise this talking point. GetApp listing
Advertising TFP terms allow advertisements in platform surfaces. Position ad‑free environment as premium, brand‑safe experience for families. TFP T&Cs §3.8
Mobile (Admin) Both offer mobile support; admin‑side native depth varies. Accelerate admin mobile improvements and highlight JoinIn + mobile registers today. JoinIn app
Settlement Speed LA offers PayPal recurring settlements in minutes; DD payouts naturally slower. Explicitly coach customers when to use each method (fast cashflow vs batch DD) and surface settlement ETAs at checkout. LA blog • Buyer Guide

Reviews & sentiments

LoveAdmin — highlights & critiques

  • Strong support and guided migrations to v2; measurable admin time savings (multiple recent reviews).
  • Occasional learning curve for new admins; some noted PayPal linking friction historically (improving on v2).
  • Parents value simple bookings and consolidated histories.

Refs: Capterra listing

TeamFeePay — highlights & critiques

  • Easy to use for members and volunteers; tightens club finances and dues collection.
  • Some reviewers cite difficulty changing plans or tracking monthly payments; some note lack of direct emailing.

Refs: SoftwareAdvice • GetApp

Sales battle cards

▶ Treasurer / Finance — cashflow, fees & risk Talk tracks + traps

Talk track

  • “Choose the right method for the right moment: instant recurring via PayPal or batch direct debit for predictable cycles.”
  • “Transparent fee view & transaction‑fee reporting means no surprises.”

Trap the competitor

  • Ask how fixed per‑transaction fees are disclosed up‑front and whether cash is held in a reserve account.
  • Confirm the size of dispute fees and who bears them.

Proof points

  • LA: PayPal recurring settles in minutes
  • LA Buyer Guide: settlement table
  • TFP T&Cs: reserve, dispute fees, fee schedule
▶ Secretary / Admin — registration, comms, time‑savings

Talk track

  • “Granular comms: target non‑openers, schedule reminders, reduce manual follow‑ups.”
  • “Unified registers & payments cut reconciliation time.”

Trap the competitor

  • Ask whether the platform supports emailing members directly and segmenting.
  • Probe how attendance lists sync with payment status.

Proof points

  • LA communications
  • LA classes & events
  • TFP reviewer mentions emailing limits
▶ Chair / Brand — family experience & trust

Talk track

  • “Ad‑free, family‑friendly experience; payments and booking flows that build trust.”
  • “Parents can self‑serve via the app and portal.”

Trap the competitor

  • Ask whether third‑party advertisements appear in the platform surfaces.

Proof points

  • LA JoinIn app
  • TFP T&Cs §3.8 advertisements
▶ Coach / Manager — simplicity on the ground

Talk track

  • “Registers, capacity and waitlists in one place; parents self‑serve booking so you can coach.”
  • “Mobile‑friendly workflows today; continued investment in admin mobile.”

Trap the competitor

  • Ask how quickly staff can check attendance and reconcile with payment status before a session.

Proof points

  • LA classes & registers
  • TFP payments & registrations
