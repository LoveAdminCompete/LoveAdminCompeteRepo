Positioning comparison

LoveAdmin

  • Comprehensive membership & club admin platform for clubs, classes, events, and sports organizations with branded booking/shop, forms, calendars, contact/CRM, payments, reporting, communications, automations, and a member app (JoinIn). [refs]
  • Emphasis on reducing admin, automated reminders/renewals, finance & business reporting, GDPR-by-design. [refs]
  • Payment module accessed via 3% platform fee (pay when you get paid); card via PayPal (settled instantly) and Direct Debit via GoCardless. [refs]

360Player

  • All-in-one sports club platform covering registrations/payments, communication, scheduling, website builder, plus sports development (video analysis, statistics, training libraries, player development). [refs]
  • Payments through Stripe and Adyen; focus on clear reporting/reconciliation and configurable payouts (typically weekly). [refs]
  • Starter plan for a whole club; add-on per-user for the “All-in-One” suite that includes performance features. [refs]

Battle Cards

LoveAdmin vs 360Player — General (Positioning)

Talk track

  • LoveAdmin is a complete operations & membership admin suite built to cut admin, grow revenue, and keep data compliant — from registrations and payments to reporting, calendars, communications, and automations — plus a member app for self-serve. [refs]
  • 360Player adds sports performance (video analysis, stats, training) on top of core club admin. For clubs prioritising operational excellence and frictionless payments/renewals, LoveAdmin is the focused fit. [refs]

Traps to avoid

  • Don’t get pulled into a “feature-count” debate around video/analytics. Reframe to admin impact, reliability, payouts, and comms.
  • Avoid promising features outside LoveAdmin’s scope (e.g., in-app video analytics). Offer integration stance instead.

Proof points

  • 3% platform fee “only when paid,” card via PayPal (settled instantly), DD via GoCardless; branded shops, flexible billing, reminders, and finance reporting. [refs]
  • Business & finance reports, communications toolkit with email/SMS/segmentation & 40+ automations; JoinIn app. [refs]
Handling “Why not 360Player for sports performance?”

Talk track

  • 360Player is strong on player development workflows (video, stats, drills). If that’s core, we can co-exist: use LoveAdmin for mission-critical ops & payments, while integrating with performance tools.
  • Where renewals, DD reliability, instant card settlements, and automated comms matter most, LoveAdmin’s admin-first design is a better operational backbone. [refs]

Traps

  • Don’t minimise their performance features — acknowledge value, then realign to operational KPIs and member experience.

Proof points

  • PayPal (card) settlements instantly; GoCardless DD supported; branded flows and robust reporting. [refs]
  • Reviews cite reduced admin workload and supportive onboarding/customer service. [refs]
