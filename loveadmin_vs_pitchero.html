Sales battle cards

1) Lead with gymnastics outcomes (progress, awards, registers, parent updates)
Talk trackTrap to avoidProof points

Talk track: “We’re built for class‑based progression. Coaches mark skills/levels in‑session, parents get branded progress emails automatically, and moving a gymnast to the next class is a two‑click flow.”

Trap: Don’t get pulled into public‑website features; pivot to class operations and family engagement.

Proof: Gymnastics page + case studies (Gemini, Garioch). Source.

2) Cash‑flow clarity (recurring cards settle in minutes; DD timelines are published)

Talk track: “For recurring cards we see funds in minutes; for Direct Debits we publish exact day‑by‑day timelines so treasurers can plan collections.”

Trap: If asked to compare exact provider fees, refer to providers’ schedules and emphasise our 3% platform fee covers full payment module, automations and support.

Proof: PayPal ‘settle in minutes’; GoCardless/L&Z timelines. Card · GC · L&Z.

3) Communications your brand owns (visual email builder + automations)

Talk track: “Branded emails with images, GIFs and attachments; 40+ automations for chasers and updates reduce manual admin.”

Trap: If compared to in‑app chat, acknowledge difference and position roadmap or integrations for urgent alerts.

Proof: Gymnastics comms section; pricing feature lists. Source.

4) Operational automation (waitlists, trials, capacity, awards, reporting)

Talk track: “Automation reduces manual processes by up to 80% in practice.”

Trap: Avoid debating public content features; refocus on admin time saved and parent experience.

Proof: LoveAdmin pricing/features list; case studies. Source.

Countering Pitchero’s fee transparency advantage

Talk track: “Our pricing is simple: 3% platform fee when you get paid, plus whatever your chosen provider charges; many clubs prefer the flexibility to select rails that maximise cash‑flow (PayPal card) or minimise fees (DD).”

Trap: Don’t speculate on providers’ fees—share links. Offer a side‑by‑side with the club’s own provider quotes.

Proof: LoveAdmin pricing · GC timeline · L&Z timeline.
