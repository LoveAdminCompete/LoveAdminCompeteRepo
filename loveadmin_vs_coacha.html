Competitive Report — LoveAdmin vs Coacha

Single‑page brief for product, marketing and sales. Focused on class‑based activity providers (with a strong lens on gymnastics values: safeguarding, progression tracking, parent experience, cash‑flow discipline).
Quick take
LoveAdmin advantages: advanced communications & automation; deep business reporting; multi‑provider payments; professional setup and consultancy.
Coacha advantages: skills/grades progression; offline front‑desk self check‑in; coach timesheets; built‑in store & B2B invoicing; transparent transaction fees.

Sales battle cards

Expandable accordions with talk tracks, traps, proof
Positioning LoveAdmin vs Coacha (for Sales)
+

Talk track: “We’re not just booking & payments. We combine branded journeys, deep automation and live insight to grow your revenue while saving time for coaches and admins.”

Traps to avoid: Don’t argue on raw price—reframe to total value: revenue lift (renewals, upsells), churn reduction via engagement, and reduced admin time.

Proof points:

  • Advanced email builder, targeted SMS/portal posts, 40+ automations【Communications & Automations】.
  • Financial & class performance reporting incl. abandoned checkout and payment requests【Business reports】.
  • Multiple payment options (card via PayPal; direct debit via GoCardless or London & Zurich)【Payments】 with automatic retries and overdue chasing.

Links: Communications · Automations · Reports · Payments

How Coacha competes (for Product & Competitive Intel)
+

Talk track: “Coacha wins with clear, low‑friction pricing and built‑ins clubs care about—progression tracking, self check‑in, coach timesheets, and a store.”

Traps to watch: Don’t underplay the importance of skill/grade progression and safeguarding workflows for youth athletics.

Proof points:

  • Skills & grades progression, safeguarding officer CC, illness reporting【Plan comparison】.
  • Front‑desk self check‑in (works offline), coach/staff timesheets【Plan comparison】.
  • Transparent transaction fees; quick settlements via Stripe/GoCardless【Pricing】.
  • Store & B2B invoicing for kit sales and invoicing schools/providers【Commerce/B2B】.
