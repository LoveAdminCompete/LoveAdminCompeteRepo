Positioning comparison

Download Detailed Report PDF

LoveAdmin

  • UK‑based platform serving sports clubs, children’s activity providers, classes & courses, membership orgs, and team sports.
  • Strengths in Direct Debit (GoCardless, London & Zurich), PayPal card-on-file for recurring payments that settle in minutes, and robust admin automation, communications, reporting and safeguarding.
  • JoinIn Online App for member self‑service; strong UK phone support hours.
Support hours (UK): Mon–Fri 9:00–5:30, phone 0330 818 8536. (See “Sources”)

iClassPro

  • US‑based platform focused on gymnastics, dance, swim and similar centres.
  • Broad suite: Class Management, Parent Portal & Branded App, Integrated Websites, Autopilot communications, POS, QuickBooks Online integration, Appointments, Parties, Skill Tracking.
  • Card processing via iClassPro Payment Services with published US fee schedules and standard/next‑day funding; UK specifics not publicly documented.
Field notes: “Support is based out of the USA”, “Iframes are tidy”, “They are very expensive”, “We struggle to win their customers.”

Reviews & sentiments comparison Capterra • GetApp • Trustpilot • Software Advice

LoveAdmin

  • Capterra: highlights easy registers and Direct Debits; strong migration to V2; support responsiveness; some learning curve; lack of native phone app noted by one reviewer; occasional PayPal linking friction. (See sources)
  • GetApp: positive admin efficiency; some onboarding time/communication concerns in individual reviews.
  • Trustpilot (claimed profile): mixed sentiment; company lists UK address + phone; LoveAdmin responds to 100% of negative reviews.

iClassPro

  • Capterra: strong for activity centres; some criticisms around regional payment integrations.
  • Software Advice: notes about credit card fees, monthly costs, and difficulty using external software for payments.
  • General: rich feature breadth (POS, branded app, skills); public pricing clarity mainly US‑focused.

Sales battle cards talk tracks • traps • proof points

When the prospect prioritises UK cashflow predictability & settlement speed
Talk track

“With LoveAdmin you choose the right rail per use‑case: Direct Debit via GoCardless/L&Z for predictable recurring collections, or PayPal card‑on‑file for fees that settle in minutes. Clubs cut reconciliation time and meet payroll with confidence.”

Trap

They ask for next‑day card funding—iClassPro references US next‑day. Clarify that UK DD timelines are clearly published and PayPal delivers same‑day funds for recurring/ad‑hoc.

Proof

Link to GoCardless & L&Z timelines + PayPal “settle in minutes” article in Sources.

When they emphasise mobile app polish
Talk track

“Parents complete tasks via the mobile‑friendly JoinIn experience with saved cards and QR check‑in. Clubs get the same conversions without app‑store friction. For branded app needs, we can explore white‑label options.”

Trap

iClassPro’s Branded App + push notifications impress visually.

Proof

Show JoinIn flows and QR attendance; highlight PayPal stored‑card for repeat purchases.

When they mention POS, inventory or pro‑shops
Talk track

“LoveAdmin online shop covers memberships, kits, tickets and rentals, with upsells and reporting. For on‑site POS, partner integrations can be recommended.”

Trap

iClassPro POS has barcode scanning and inventory built‑in.

Proof

Show Buyer’s Guide shop & stock; Business Reports for sales and stock insights.

When skills/progress visuals are must‑have
Talk track

“We track attendance and performance, and can export/share progress. We’re exploring templated ‘skills’ frameworks for coaches.”

Trap

iClassPro Skills module demos can look compelling for gymnastics.

Proof

Show registers with consent/medical flags and performance notes; roadmap outline.
