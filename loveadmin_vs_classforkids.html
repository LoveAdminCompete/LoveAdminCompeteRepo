Positioning comparison

LoveAdmin

All‑in‑oneGrowth consultancyMarketing school

LoveAdmin is positioned as an all‑in‑one membership and booking platform for activity providers. It covers the full journey from registrations and payments to calendars, registers, communications and reporting. It also offers value‑added programmes like Step2Success (£4,000 consultancy) and the School of Marketing to drive growth and operational excellence.

Gymnastics angle: Emphasis on safeguarding, progress tracking, capacity and competition/event management.

ClassForKids (Class4Kids)

SimplicityUpfront paymentsKids’ clubs focus

ClassForKids is a booking and management system oriented toward children’s clubs. It stresses simple parent booking flows (book in a few clicks), automated invoicing and subscriptions, and offline‑capable registers for coaches.

High user ratings reported on review sites
Time‑savings via upfront payment enforcement

Reviews & sentiment (summary)

LoveAdmin

  • Strengths Comprehensive feature set; helpful support; time savings via automation; V2 UX improvements; good DD (GoCardless) integration.
  • Mixed Some migration pains from legacy V1; occasional support delays; optional extras discoverability for parents.
  • Weakness No full admin mobile app yet; some PayPal workflows feel clunky for a few users.

ClassForKids

  • Strengths Very easy to use; quick parent bookings; upfront payments improve cash flow; offline‑capable registers; helpful support.
  • Mixed Simple reports; constraints for discounts/subscriptions; limited attachment sending.
  • Weakness Higher effective fees (platform + Stripe); no calendar view; limited progress‑tracking; some cancellation friction reported by parents.

Sales battle cards

Positioning & outcomes›

Talk track: LoveAdmin is an all‑in‑one platform spanning bookings, payments, registers, communications, reporting, plus growth consultancy and marketing education.

Trap to set: Ask prospects how they’ll grow memberships and revenue beyond “software”. If they mention extra support, highlight Step2Success and the School of Marketing.

Proof points: Step2Success (£4k consultancy), free School of Marketing courses, sport‑specific communities.

Payments, fees & settlement›

Talk track: Choice of payment provider, automated retries, partial refunds/credits, and fast settlement (PayPal in minutes). Reports are actionable.

Trap to set: Confirm total cost of ClassForKids (licence + 3.1% platform + Stripe 1.5%+20p). This can approach ~4.5% in practice.

Proof points: LoveAdmin pricing page; C4K pricing/G‑Cloud documents; payment solutions page.

Registers, safeguarding & progress›

Talk track: Custom registers (trialist, injured), QR check‑in, consent/medical icons, progress‑tracking for skills/levels—vital for disciplined coaching.

Trap to set: Ask how they track skill progress today. If “notes”, stress the benefits of structured progression and reporting.

Proof points: LoveAdmin activity calendars & development programmes.

Reporting & analytics›

Talk track: Move beyond “lists”. Actionable reports show payments, late payers, cancelled DDs, campaign engagement—with one‑click reminders or invoices.

Trap to set: Ask how they optimise schedules or pricing. Simple reports rarely suffice when scaling.

Proof points: Business reports pages; comms tracking.
