Positioning

LoveAdmin Narrative

  • All‑in‑one admin system for clubs, classes & membership orgs with payments, bookings, comms, reporting and more.
  • Claims to reduce manual processes by up to 80% via automations and workflows (e.g., chasing late payments).
  • Offers JoinIn member app, School of Marketing content, and growth consultancy.

ClassForKids Narrative

  • Booking & management platform tailored to kids clubs; strong in registers, parent comms, bookings & payments.
  • Supports subscription (recurring card) billing; offline registers; “Payment Secures Booking” option.
  • Actively markets coaching/enablement for growth and offers price‑match on fees in T&Cs.
