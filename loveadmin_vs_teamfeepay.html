Positioning comparison

LoveAdmin

  • All‑in‑one club & membership management platform for sports clubs, classes/courses, and membership orgs; supports Direct Debit (GoCardless), card & PayPal for one‑off and recurring payments, with automated schedules, reminders and retries. Ref: pricing page & football page
  • Transparent service fee with a platform fee on collected payments; clear setup/implementation details. Ref: pricing
  • Depth in admin efficiency: pro‑rata billing, instalments, product relationships, award schemes, automated waitlists, and granular comms (email/SMS/WhatsApp). Ref: Buyer Guide PDF

TeamFeePay

  • Football‑specific platform focused on payments & registrations, fundraising, e‑commerce (club shop), and club development support (webinars, funding advice, facilities). Ref: homepage & pillar pages
  • Simple Annual Licence fee; payment processing fees also apply; claims instant payment collections and full reporting. Ref: pricing & payments pages
  • Fully account‑managed service; emphasis on increasing club income and reducing admin by bespoke plans. Ref: site copy

Sales Battle Cards

Positioning & Discovery
Use these to qualify quickly and steer the convo toward LoveAdmin strengths.
  • Ask “Are you looking for a football‑only system or a platform used across multiple sports and membership orgs?”
  • Talk Track LoveAdmin supports Direct Debit via GoCardless and instant card/PayPal settlements via PayPal, with rich admin automation and reporting. This helps volunteers minimise chasing and improve cash‑flow predictability.
  • Trap If they emphasise webinars/funding advice and a fully account‑managed service, acknowledge that TFP leans into club development services; then pivot to LoveAdmin’s deeper built‑in payment, comms, and reporting feature set.
  • Proof Instant card settlement via PayPal; Direct Debit timelines published; transparent 3% platform fee + clear service fee schedule.
Payments & Cashflow
  • Talk Track “We combine instant card settlements (PayPal) with bank‑friendly Direct Debit (GoCardless). You choose rails per product—e.g., memberships by Direct Debit, merch by card for faster cashflow.”
  • Proof PayPal instant settlement; GoCardless 5–7 working days; L&Z Direct Debit 7–11 working days (published). Payment retries, overdue reminders, and refunds handled centrally.
  • Trap TFP markets “instant collections” but does not publish bank settlement timings or detailed processing fee tables publicly; push for written SLAs and fee schedules.
  • Question “Do you need to reconcile PayPal/Direct Debit settlement schedules by product to match venue/camp pre‑payments?”
Registrations, Comms & Admin
  • Talk Track LoveAdmin offers age‑validated registrations, product relationships (enforce kit with membership), pro‑rata, instalments, automated waitlists, and granular comms (email/SMS/WhatsApp/templates/scheduling).
  • Proof Buyer Guide PDF feature set; Help Centre articles for billing options & notifications.
  • Trap TFP emphasises ease, but specific details for registers/attendance and advanced comms are not fully documented on its site—ask to see feature depth.
Value & Pricing
  • Talk Track LoveAdmin pricing is transparent (£20/mo Sports Clubs; 3% platform fee; published setup). TFP lists a licence at £41.99/mo or £416.58/yr plus processing fees; its Terms reference additional fees (e.g., disputes up to £50).
  • Trap If TFP claims “free to launch”, clarify the ongoing licence + processing fees; request the full processing schedule and settlement SLAs.
  • Proof Cite vendor pricing pages & Terms.
